Condensate Polishing Unit (CPU)

A Condensate Polishing Unit (CPU) is an essential water treatment system designed to purify and recover condensate water in power plants and industrial settings. Our advanced CPUs effectively remove dissolved and suspended impurities, ensuring high-quality water for boiler feed and other critical applications.

Superior Filtration

Our CPUs feature multi-stage filtration systems that effectively remove particulate matter, dissolved solids, and other contaminants.

Ion Exchange Technology

Advanced ion exchange resins ensure complete removal of ionic impurities, resulting in ultra-pure water suitable for high-pressure boilers.

Automated Operation

Fully automated control systems with real-time monitoring capabilities for reliable and efficient operation.

Energy Efficiency

Designed to optimize energy usage while maximizing water recovery rates, reducing operational costs.

Our Condensate Polishing Units are custom-designed to meet specific industrial requirements, offering reliable performance and long service life. Contact us to learn more about how our CPUs can benefit your operation.
